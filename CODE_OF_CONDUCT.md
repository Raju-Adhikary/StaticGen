## Code of Conduct

### 1. Respect and Professionalism

Treat everyone with respect. Communicate clearly and constructively. Disagreements are fine. Personal attacks are not.

### 2. Inclusive Environment

Foster a welcoming space for people of all backgrounds, identities, and experience levels. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.

### 3. Constructive Contributions

Provide feedback that improves the project. Focus on the work, not the person. Keep discussions relevant and solution-oriented.

### 4. Accountability

Own your actions and their impact. If you make a mistake, acknowledge it and correct it.

### 5. Collaboration Over Ego

Prioritize the health of the project over personal preferences. Major changes should be discussed before implementation.

### 6. Compliance

Follow applicable laws, licenses, and project guidelines.

---

Failure to follow this code may result in warnings, restricted participation, or removal from the project.
Keep it professional. Keep it respectful.
